I fell in love with technology in 6th grade. This was my first time having a class that taught students general programming knowledge. I got to program a robot which was what made me realize what I wanted to do when I grew up. I have played 5 different sports being: Baseball (from age 6), Flag Football (just one year), Soccer (for 2 years), Basketball (for 3 years), and Tennis (since middle school). My first job was at Meijer. I worked at Meijer for 10 months as a cart pusher. I would help out in other areas from time to time. My favorite color is blue as you can tell from the website's color scheme. I do wish I had the opportunity learn to code at a much younger age. I did not start exploring the world of programming until I got my first All-In-One the summer before my freshmen year. Even then, it was not until my junior year in school that I really focused on coding and learning all I could. Minecraft became a tool that ignited that spark to want to learn to code.

I love to learn new things. Something that I recently enjoyed learning about was Godot. Making my own game, Endless Blade, was a lot of fun. I learned a lot about how important hierarchy of how nodes are placed can quite literally make or break your game. I still can't figure out why one computer is having graphical issues, but everything else is fine.

Once in a while I like to improve older projects that I worked on. One of them being my old blackjack game. My color scheme was not the best. I used to many blues I am now realizing.
